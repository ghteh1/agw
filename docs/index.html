01 — About the company

A family business, rooted in six decades of craft.

Asia Green Wood Sdn. Bhd. — formerly Asia Plywood Company Sdn. Bhd. — was founded in 1964. Under the leadership of Dato' Tan Boon Huat, who took over management in 1995, it grew into a family business built on integrity and trust, expanding its footprint across Europe, Asia and America.

In 2023, newly appointed CEO Tan Loong Khoon (second generation) pivoted the company toward a greener vision, integrating sustainability into our core operations. With decades of manufacturing expertise behind us, we have evolved from a plywood manufacturer into a comprehensive enterprise managing forest concessions and renewable energy — moving toward a more sustainable future, filled with love and nature.

Vision

To lead a new era of timber — where love guides our work, nature is our partner, and sustainability becomes a way of life.

Mission

To renew the bond between people and nature — through how we work, the care we give, and the love we share.

02 / Sustainability

Sustainability, built into every step.

From forest to finished panel
01

100% sustainable sources

Every log is 100% traceable to a responsibly managed forest, fully aligned with the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR).

02

Sustainability, engineered into our factory

At Asia Green Wood, sustainability is built into the factory itself. We have fitted as much rooftop solar as the available roof area allows — currently supplying 15–25% of our electricity — and run dust-extraction systems, low-emission E1 adhesives and LED lighting throughout. Glue-contaminated wastewater is never released into public irrigation canals; it is treated to remove harmful resins and formaldehyde, while production residues are separated and recycled rather than sent to landfill.

Syngas boiler, biochar & a circular economy

Our advanced syngas boiler and biochar facility converts our own wood waste into clean electricity — supplying 70–75% of our total electricity and savings while reducing carbon by up to 90%. The biochar it produces becomes a high-value co-product for road base, concrete and sustainable agriculture, closing the loop on waste and creating a new revenue stream within a circular, low-carbon economy.

03 / Process

From standing tree to finished panel — five stages.

~18 working days · end-to-end
01

Sourcing

Log selection from legally verified, responsibly managed concessions across peninsular Malaysia and Sabah.

02

Peeling & drying

Logs are rotary-peeled into veneer and kiln-dried to 6–10% moisture on Japanese drying lines.

03

Glue & lay-up

Cross-laminated in odd-number plies with E0 / E1 / WBP adhesive systems, matched to end-use.

04

Hot press & cure

Panels are pressed, cured and trimmed. Each is stamped with batch, grade and moisture values.

05

Grade & ship

Seasoned graders sort to BB-face / A-grade. Pallets are strapped, fumigated and containerised.
