01 / About Us

About Asia Green Wood

Three words · one philosophy
Our Name

ASIA

Represents Asia's ancient heritage of coexisting harmoniously with forests and wood. By embracing wood, we reconnect with nature and revive a timeless cultural relationship between humanity and the forest — sharing this wisdom with the world.

GREEN

Symbolises our commitment to sustainability, green energy and environmental stewardship. It reflects our vision of nurturing a world that functions like a living garden — where forests thrive, ecosystems are respected, and growth is balanced with responsibility.

WOOD

Embodies the promotion of wood culture worldwide. Wood is a natural resource meant to be used, yet treated with respect and responsibility. Through mindful and sustainable use, we honour nature while ensuring forests continue to flourish for future generations.

06 / History

A legacy of growth & responsibility.

1964 → 2026
1964

The Foundation

The Legacy Begins: Asia Plywood Company was incorporated in Sungai Petani, establishing a vital foothold in the Malaysian timber industry.

1995

The Strategic Transition

Modernization: Under the leadership of Mr. Tan Boon Huat, the company underwent a comprehensive management transformation, ushering in a new era of structural growth and operational modernization.

2005

Global Footprint & Sustainability

Market Expansion: Successfully executed our inaugural shipment to the UK, marking the start of our global expansion strategy.

Sustainability Milestones: Achieved accreditation from FSC and MTCS, with the latter receiving official PEFC endorsement in May 2009.

2009

Technological Leap

Facility Advancement: Relocated to a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Bukit Selambau, Kedah.

Precision Engineering: Integrated high-tech Japanese machinery and an optimized floor plan to meet premium "export-grade" standards.

2017 – 2018

Market Diversification

Japan (2017): Attained JAS Standards certification, triggering a significant expansion into the Japanese market.

USA (2018): Attained CARB certification, Uplifted product quality to successfully introduce high-grade overlay plywood to the United States.

2023

The Next Generation

Leadership Succession: Mr. LK Tan assumed the role of CEO, spearheading the company's evolution for the next generation.

2024

The Shift to Renewables

Solar Integration: Installed a rooftop solar array, generating 20% of the factory's electricity needs and marking our first major step toward environmental stewardship.

2025

Strategic Rebranding

Asia Green Wood: Formally transitioned from Asia Plywood to Asia Green Wood Sdn Bhd. This rebranding signifies our commitment to a broader "Green" vision within the Asia Green Group.

2026

Carbon Neutrality & Traceability

The Mission to Decarbonize: Installed advanced gasifier and waste boiler systems to convert wood waste into energy, reducing our carbon footprint by 80%.

Future-Proof Compliance: Achieved full alignment with EUDR (EU Deforestation Regulation) standards, ensuring total traceability and continued access to global markets.
